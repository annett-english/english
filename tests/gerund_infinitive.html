1. Gerund после предлогов

in / for / at / of + V-ing

I'm interested in learning English.

Поставьте глагол в скобках в форму герундия (V+ing).

  1. I'm interested in (learn) English.
    Ответ: learning
  2. She's really good at (paint) portraits.
    Ответ: painting
  3. Thank you for (help) me move the sofa.
    Ответ: helping
  4. He's afraid of (fly), so he always travels by train.
    Ответ: flying
  5. We're excited about (start) the new project.
    Ответ: starting
  6. They talked about (move) to Berlin next year.
    Ответ: moving
  7. I'm tired of (wait) for the bus every morning.
    Ответ: waiting
  8. She apologized for (be) late to the meeting.
    Ответ: being
  9. He's used to (work) night shifts.
    Ответ: working
  10. We look forward to (see) you at the wedding.
    Ответ: seeing

2. Gerund в роли подлежащего

V-ing + verb

Reading is relaxing.

Выберите правильную форму, которая стоит в начале предложения (в роли подлежащего).

  1. ___ keeps you fit and improves your mood.

    Ответ: Swimming

  2. ___ a new language takes patience and daily practice.

    Ответ: Learning

  3. ___ every day improves your vocabulary and memory.

    Ответ: Reading

  4. ___ too much coffee can cause anxiety.

    Ответ: Drinking

  5. ___ in public requires a lot of confidence.

    Ответ: Speaking

  6. ___ new recipes is one of my favourite hobbies.

    Ответ: Trying

  7. ___ money wisely is an important life skill.

    Ответ: Spending

  8. ___ a bike is great cardio exercise.

    Ответ: Riding

Rephrase: перепишите предложение так, чтобы герундий стал подлежащим (V-ing at the start). Пример: It's easy to learn. → Learning is easy.

  1. It's dangerous to drive fast.→

    Ответ: Driving fast is dangerous.

  2. It's expensive to travel by plane.→

    Ответ: Travelling by plane is expensive.

  3. It's fun to swim in the sea.→

    Ответ: Swimming in the sea is fun.

  4. It's boring to wait in long queues.→

    Ответ: Waiting in long queues is boring.

  5. It's healthy to eat vegetables every day.→

    Ответ: Eating vegetables every day is healthy.

  6. It's difficult to learn a new language.→

    Ответ: Learning a new language is difficult.

  7. It's relaxing to walk in the park.→

    Ответ: Walking in the park is relaxing.

  8. It's important to save money.→

    Ответ: Saving money is important.

3. Gerund после некоторых глаголов

enjoy / avoid / finish / suggest ...

I enjoy reading before bed.

  1. I enjoy (read) detective novels.
    Ответ: reading
  2. She avoids (eat) junk food before workouts.
    Ответ: eating
  3. They finished (write) the report at midnight.
    Ответ: writing
  4. We suggest (go) to the cinema tonight.
    Ответ: going
  5. He finally admitted (steal) the money.
    Ответ: stealing
  6. She misses (live) in her hometown.
    Ответ: living
  7. He practises (speak) English every day.
    Ответ: speaking
  8. I don't mind (wait) a little longer.
    Ответ: waiting
  9. They kept (ask) the same question.

  10. We spent hours (clean) the garage.

4. Gerund: отрицание

not + V-ing

She admitted not telling the truth.

Заполните пропуск отрицательной формой герундия (not + V-ing).

  1. She admitted (not / tell) the truth.
    Ответ: not telling
  2. He apologized for (not / call) me earlier.
    Ответ: not calling
  3. I regret (not / study) harder at school.
    Ответ: not studying
  4. He denied (not / lock) the door.
    Ответ: not locking
  5. I feel bad about (not / invite) her to the party.
    Ответ: not inviting
  6. She regrets (not / apply) for that job.
    Ответ: not applying
  7. They admitted (not / finish) the project on time.
    Ответ: not finishing
  8. He mentioned (not / sleep) well last night.
    Ответ: not sleeping

5. Infinitive после прилагательных

easy / hard / important + to+V

It's easy to learn.

Поставьте глагол в скобках в форму инфинитива (to + V).

  1. It's easy (learn) the basics of chess.
    Ответ: to learn
  2. It was difficult (find) a taxi at night.
    Ответ: to find
  3. It's important (drink) enough water every day.
    Ответ: to drink
  4. It's dangerous (swim) in this river after the storm.
    Ответ: to swim
  5. It's necessary (bring) your passport to the airport.
    Ответ: to bring
  6. It's impossible (finish) this report today.
    Ответ: to finish
  7. It's kind of you (help) your neighbours.
    Ответ: to help
  8. It's exciting (travel) to new countries.
    Ответ: to travel
  9. It's rude (interrupt) people while they're talking.
    Ответ: to interrupt

6. Infinitive, чтобы показать цель

to+V = "чтобы"

I study to pass the exam.

Заполните пропуск инфинитивом цели (to + V).

  1. I study English every evening (get) a better job.
    Ответ: to get
  2. She went to the shop (buy) some milk.
    Ответ: to buy
  3. He turned on the radio (listen) to the news.
    Ответ: to listen
  4. They saved money for two years (travel) around Europe.
    Ответ: to travel
  5. She called the doctor (make) an appointment.
    Ответ: to make
  6. He left home early (avoid) the morning traffic.
    Ответ: to avoid
  7. We opened the window (get) some fresh air.
    Ответ: to get
  8. I set an alarm (wake) up on time.
    Ответ: to wake
  9. They hired a local guide (explore) the old city safely.
    Ответ: to explore

9. Verb + person + to + Verb

  2. I want you (help) me carry this box.
    Ответ: to help
  3. He asked her (close) the window, it was cold.

    Ответ: to join

    Ответ: to study
  6. The teacher told the students (be) quiet.
    Ответ: to be
  7. I'd like you (meet) my sister.
    Ответ: to meet
  8. She asked us (not / make) any noise.

  9. He wants everyone (arrive) on time.

11. Writing & Speaking

на проверку учителю

Свободные задания без автопроверки — работу нужно отправить учителю самостоятельно.

✍️ Writing. Напишите 5–8 предложений о своих планах на будущее. Используйте минимум 3 герундия (после enjoy, avoid, suggest, keep, admit...) и 3 инфинитива (после want, decide, hope, promise... или "verb + person + to+V").

🎙️ Speaking. Запишите голосовое сообщение (1–2 минуты): расскажите о своём хобби — что вам нравится и не нравится делать, о планах и намерениях. Используйте герундий и инфинитив.
