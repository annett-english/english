Правда или ложь?

Прослушайте/прочитайте текст ещё раз и отметьте, верны ли утверждения.

Phil says he never sits down and exercises every day.

Charlotte Edwardson is a professor at Leicester University.

When we sit, our blood circulation improves.

Standing uses about 20% more energy than sitting.

On average, British adults sit for seven hours a day.

1. False — he says he does spend a lot of time sitting down.
2. True.
3. False — circulation reduces, not improves.
4. True — standing uses ~20% more energy; walking uses ~92% more.
5. False — the real average is nine hours (Phil's guess of seven was wrong).
