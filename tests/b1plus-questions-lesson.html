PART 1

Indirect questions — правило

📌Зачем используем

Косвенные вопросы звучат вежливее, чем прямые. Так мы обращаемся к незнакомым людям, в магазине, на ресепшене, по телефону.

Excuse me, could you tell me where the station is?

1Полезные фразы для начала

Can you tell me…Could you tell me…Do you know…Do you think…Do you remember…Would you mind telling me…

2Порядок слов — утвердительный!

После вводной фразы идёт прямой порядок слов: подлежащее + глагол. Вопросительный порядок (инверсию) не используем.

Can you tell me where it is? Can you tell me where is it?

3Убираем do / does / did

Вспомогательные do / does / did исчезают, а глагол меняет форму: does he live → he lives, did she go → she went.

Do you know where does he live? Do you know where he lives?

4if / whether

Если в прямом вопросе нет вопросительного слова (это yes/no вопрос), соединяем части через if или whether.

Do you know if he is coming? Do you know whether he is coming?

5Question words

Если вопросительное слово есть (what, where, when, why, how…), именно оно соединяет две части предложения.

Could you tell me what time the shop opens?

6Direct vs Indirect

Direct: What time does the shop open?
Indirect: Could you tell me what time it opens? ← did/does ушли, порядок прямой

7Другие выражения (тот же порядок слов)

I wonder…I was wondering…I'm not sure…I can't remember…I'd like to know…
I'd like to know what time you're coming home.
EX A1

Выбери правильный вариант

В каждом предложении выбери версию с правильным порядком слов.

  1. Do you know where ?
    ✔ …where he lives?
  2. Can you tell me what time ?
    ✔ …what time the film starts?
  3. Could you tell me she is coming to the meeting?
    ✔ …if she is coming… (нет question word → if/whether)
  4. I wonder .
    ✔ I wonder where the station is.
  5. Do you remember ?
    ✔ …what he said? (did исчезает → said)
  6. Would you mind telling me how much ?
    ✔ …how much it costs?
EX A2

if / whether или question word?

Впиши в пропуск if / whether или подходящее вопросительное слово (where, when, how, why, what).

  1. Do you know the bank opens on Saturdays?
    ✔ if / whether
  2. Can you tell me the toilets are?
    ✔ where
  3. I'd like to know this bus goes to the centre.
    ✔ if / whether
  4. Do you remember her birthday is?
    ✔ when
  5. I'm not sure he's married.
    ✔ if / whether
  6. Could you tell me much this jacket costs?
    ✔ how
  7. I wonder she left so early — she seemed happy.
    ✔ why
EX A3

Rephrase: direct → indirect

Преврати прямой вопрос в косвенный. Начало уже написано — допиши остальное.

  1. Where is the station?
    Can you tell me
    ✔ Can you tell me where the station is?
  2. What time does the shop open?
    Do you know
    ✔ Do you know what time the shop opens?
  3. Does he work here?
    Do you know
    ✔ Do you know if / whether he works here?
  4. Where did she go?
    I wonder
    ✔ I wonder where she went. (did ушёл → went)
  5. How much is it?
    Could you tell me
    ✔ Could you tell me how much it is?
  6. Is Anna coming to the party?
    Do you know
    ✔ Do you know if / whether Anna is coming to the party?
  7. Why is he late?
    I'd like to know
    ✔ I'd like to know why he is late.
EX A4

Найди и исправь ошибку

В каждом предложении есть ошибка. Напиши предложение правильно целиком.

  1. Do you know where does he live?
    ✔ Do you know where he lives?
  2. Can you tell me what time is it?
    ✔ Can you tell me what time it is?
  3. I wonder if is she at home.
    ✔ I wonder if she is at home.
  4. Could you tell me where did you buy it?
    ✔ Could you tell me where you bought it? (did ушёл → bought)
  5. I'm not sure if does this train stop in Bologna.
    ✔ I'm not sure if this train stops in Bologna.
PART 2

Direct questions — правило

1Вопросы со вспомогательными и модальными

Ставим вспомогательный / модальный глагол перед подлежащим (инверсия). В Present / Past Simple добавляем do / does / did.

How long have you been waiting? Can you help me? Did you see him?

2Отрицательные вопросы

Используем, чтобы выразить удивление или проверить информацию.

Isn't this a beautiful place? Why didn't you like the film?

3Предлог — в конец вопроса

Предлоги (about, for, with, from…) ставим в конец. Фраза «What about…?» очень частая.

What are they talking about? About what are they talking?

4Who / What = подлежащее (subject questions)

Если who / what — само подлежащее вопроса, то do / does / did не нужны — глагол идёт сразу после.

Who lives in that house? Who wrote this? What happened? Who has done it? How many people work there?
EX B1

Вставь вспомогательный глагол

Впиши подходящее слово: do / does / did / is / are / were / have / can.

  1. you like coffee?
    ✔ Do
  2. she working right now?
    ✔ Is
  3. How long you been waiting?
    ✔ have
  4. you see him yesterday?
    ✔ Did
  5. you help me with this bag, please?
    ✔ Can / Could
  6. Where they going when you saw them?
    ✔ were
  7. What time the museum open?
    ✔ does
EX B2

Отрицательные вопросы

Вырази удивление: впиши отрицательную форму (isn't, aren't, didn't, hasn't, don't…).

  1. this a beautiful place? Look at that view!
    ✔ Isn't
  2. Why you like the film? Everyone loved it!
    ✔ didn't
  3. you coming with us? We're leaving now!
    ✔ Aren't
  4. he finished yet? It's been three hours!
    ✔ Hasn't
  5. you remember me? We met last summer!
    ✔ Don't
EX B3

Предлог в конец

Заверши вопрос подходящим предлогом (about, for, with, from, to, at).

  1. What are they talking ?
    ✔ about
  2. Who are you waiting ?
    ✔ for
  3. What are you looking ?
    ✔ at / for
  4. Where are you ?
    ✔ from
  5. What is this song ?
    ✔ about
  6. Who did you go to the concert ?
    ✔ with
  7. Who were you talking on the phone?
    ✔ to
EX B4

Who / What = подлежащее?

Выбери правильный вариант. Помни: если who/what — подлежащее, do/does/did не нужны.

  1. Who this book?
    ✔ Who wrote this book?
  2. What last night?
    ✔ What happened last night?
  3. Who in that house?
    ✔ Who lives in that house?
  4. How many people there?
    ✔ How many people work there?
  5. Who you last night? — Tom did.
    ✔ Who called you? (who = подлежащее)
  6. Who last night? — I called Tom.
    ✔ Who did you call? (who = дополнение → нужен did)
  7. Who it?
    ✔ Who has done it?
EX B5

MIX: все типы вопросов

Допиши вопрос к ответу или по заданию. Начало уже написано.

  1. — She lives in Kraków.
    Where
    ✔ Where does she live?
  2. — They arrived at 6 o'clock.
    What time
    ✔ What time did they arrive?
  3. — Tom broke the cup.
    Who
    ✔ Who broke the cup? (без did!)
  4. — They're talking about football.
    What
    ✔ What are they talking about? (предлог в конце)
  5. Сделай вежливым: Where is the bank?
    Could you tell me
    ✔ Could you tell me where the bank is?
  6. Сделай вежливым: Does this bus go to the airport?
    Do you know
    ✔ Do you know if this bus goes to the airport?
  7. Удивись: твой друг не оценил фильм, который все любят.
    Why
    ✔ Why didn't you like it / the film?
  8. Сделай вежливым: How long have you been waiting?
    I was wondering
    ✔ I was wondering how long you have been waiting.
