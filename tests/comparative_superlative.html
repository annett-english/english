📖 Вспомним правила

⚖️ Comparative — сравниваем два предмета/человека: adjective -er / more + adjective + than.
My cat is bigger than your cat. This film is more interesting than that one.

🏆 Superlative — «самый-самый» в группе: the + adjective-est / the most + adjective.
It's the oldest bridge in the world. She's the best student in the class.

  • 🌍 С местами используем in: the biggest city in Poland (❌ of the world).
  • ⭐ Исключения: good → better → the best, bad → worse → the worst, far → farther → the farthest.
