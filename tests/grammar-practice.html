01

Present Simple (verb)

Когда используем
1. Привычки: She drinks coffee every morning.
2. Факты: Water boils at 100°C.
3. Регулярные действия: He goes to the gym three times a week.
Форма
I/You/We/They work. He/She/It works.
I/You/We/They don't work. He/She/It doesn't work.
Do you work? Does he work?
Упражнения
1. She tea every day. (drink) → drinks
2. Water at 0°C. (freeze) → freezes
3. My parents in Warsaw. (not / live) → don't live
4. Do you on Saturdays? (work) → work
02

Present Simple (to be)

Когда используем
Возраст: She is 25 years old.
Профессия: She is a teacher.
Чувства: They are happy.
Форма
I am. He/She/It is. You/We/They are.
I am not. He/She/It isn't. You/We/They aren't.
Am I…? Is he/she/it…? Are you/we/they…?
Упражнения
1. She 25 years old. → is
2. I a teacher. → am
3. They happy about the news. → are
4. he a doctor? → is
03

Present Continuous

Когда используем
1. Действие в моменте: She is cooking dinner now.
2. Временная ситуация: I am staying with my friend this week.
Форма
Subject + am/is/are + V-ing → She is reading.
Subject + am/is/are not + V-ing → She isn't reading.
Am/Is/Are + subject + V-ing? → Is she reading?
Упражнения
1. She dinner now. (cook) → is cooking
2. I with my friend this week. (stay) → am staying
3. Look! It . (rain) → is raining
4. He at the moment. (not / work) → isn't working
04

Present Continuous (future)

Когда используем
Планы на будущее: We are meeting our friends tomorrow.
Сегодня я работаю в офисе (настоящее), а в отпуске я работаю у моря (будущее).
Форма
Subject + am/is/are + V-ing → I'm meeting Tom tomorrow.
Subject + am/is/are not + V-ing
Am/Is/Are + subject + V-ing?
Упражнения
1. We our friends tomorrow. (meet) → are meeting
2. I to Paris next Monday. (fly) → am flying
3. Are you anything special this weekend? (do) → doing
05

Past Simple (verb)

Когда используем
Законченное действие в прошлом: yesterday, last week, ago.
She visited London last year.
Форма
Subject + V2/-ed → I played. She went.
Subject + didn't + V1 → He didn't play.
Did + subject + V1? → Did you play?
Упражнения
1. She London last year. (visit) → visited
2. He to school by bus. (go) → went
3. They the movie. (not / see) → didn't see
4. Did you TV last night? (watch) → watch
06

Past Simple (to be)

Когда используем
Возраст: She was 25 years old.
Профессия: She was a teacher.
Чувства: They were happy.
Форма
I/He/She/It was. You/We/They were.
I/He/She/It wasn't. You/We/They weren't.
Was he…? Were they…?
Упражнения
1. She 25 years old then. → was
2. They tired after the trip. → were
3. I happy with the result. → wasn't
4. he a teacher before? → was
07

Past Continuous

Когда используем
1. Действие в процессе в определённый момент прошлого: She was reading a book at 8 p.m. yesterday.
2. Прерванное действие (Past Simple прерывает): I was sleeping when the phone rang.
Форма
Subject + was/were + V-ing → I was watching TV.
Subject + was/were not + V-ing → I wasn't watching.
Was/Were + subject + V-ing? → Were you watching TV?
Упражнения
1. She a book at 8 p.m. yesterday. (read) → was reading
2. I when the phone rang. (sleep) → was sleeping
3. They TV when he arrived. (watch) → were watching
4. What were you at this time yesterday? (do) → doing
08

Present Perfect

Когда используем
1. Опыт: I have visited Italy three times.
2. Результат сейчас: I've cut my finger. / I've washed the car — it's clean.
3. Связь с настоящим: I've known my friend for 5 years.
Форма
Subject + have/has + V3 → I have finished.
Subject + haven't/hasn't + V3 → She hasn't finished.
Have/Has + subject + V3? → Have you finished?
Упражнения
1. I Italy three times. (visit) → have visited
2. She her finger. (cut) → has cut
3. I my friend for 5 years. (know) → have known
4. Have you ever to Japan? (be) → been
mix

Все времена вместе

Здесь времена перемешаны — сначала реши, какое время нужно (смотри на слова-подсказки: every morning, tomorrow, last weekend, when the phone rang, three times…), и только потом ставь глагол в нужную форму.
Упражнения
1. She coffee every morning. (drink) → drinks
2. Look! It again. (rain) → is raining
3. He usually at home, but today he in the office. (work / work) → works / is working
4. We our friends tomorrow evening. (meet) → are meeting
5. I Italy three times. (visit) → have visited
6. She her grandmother last weekend. (visit) → visited
7. They TV when the phone rang. (watch) → were watching
8. He 10 years old when his family moved to Kraków. → was
9. More and more people AI nowadays. (use) → are using
10. Have you ever to Japan? (be) → been
