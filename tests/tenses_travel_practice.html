EX 5История «Our Trip Abroad»

Прочитай историю и поставь глаголы в скобках в нужную форму. Здесь встречаются все шесть времён.

Last summer my family (decide) to go abroad. We usually (spend) our holidays at home, but that year we (want) something new. We (book) our flights online two months in advance and (rent) a small apartment near the sea.

Every morning we (go) swimming, and in the afternoon we (explore) the city or (buy) souvenirs. One day, while we (walk) in the mountains, we (meet) a group of tourists who (go) camping.

Now I'm at home again. I (already / show) my photos to all my friends, but I (not / print) them yet. Next July we (travel) to Italy — we (already / book) the hotel!
