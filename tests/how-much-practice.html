English practice · At the market

How much is it? How much does it cost?

Тренируем вопросы о цене и весе: где нужен do/does, где is/are, и когда глаголу нужна -s. Плюс хитрые выражения: a kilo, on the weekend, how long, it takes… 🍐

Способ 1 · с глаголом cost / weigh

How much does this apple cost? → It costs 2 euros.

How much do these vegetables cost? → They cost 5 euros.

How much does it weigh? → It weighs 2 kilos.

В вопросе с do/does глагол БЕЗ -s. В ответе it → +s!

Способ 2 · с to be

How much is this apple? → It is 2 euros.

How much are these vegetables? → They are 5 euros.

How much is it? → It is 20 euros.

Здесь НЕТ слова cost — только is/are.

1

do / does / is / are · Выбери

Смотри на конец вопроса: есть cost или weigh? → нужен do/does. Нет глагола? → нужен is/are.

1. How much these vegetables cost?
2. How much this apple cost?
3. How much these vegetables?
4. How much this apple?
5. How much it?
6. How much these pears weigh?
7. How much this watermelon weigh?
8. How much a bottle of milk?

2

+s или без s?

Нажми +s, если глаголу нужна -s, или без s. Помни: it → costs, weighs, takes; they → cost, weigh.

1. It cost 20 euros.
2. They cost 2 euros a kilo.
3. This apple weigh 200 grams.
4. These vegetables weigh 3 kilos.
5. It take 5 minutes to get to school.
6. My mum buy pears two times a week.
7. I eat an apple every day.
8. How much does it cost? (после does глагол без -s!)

3

Answer! · Ответь на вопрос

Посмотри на ценник и впиши глагол: is, are, costs, cost, weighs или weigh.

How much is this apple?

It 2 euros.

How much do these pears cost?

They 3 euros a kilo.

How much does this watermelon weigh?

It 4 kilos.

How much are these vegetables?

They 5 euros.

How much does a bottle of water cost?

It 1 euro.

How much do these apples weigh?

They 400 grams.

4

a или the?

📌 How much is a liter of milk? · 2 euros a kilo (= за один кило) · two times a week (= в неделю). Здесь всегда a, the!
1. How much is liter of milk?
2. How much is bottle of juice?
3. Pears cost 2 euros kilo.
4. I go to the market two times week.
5. We eat fish once month.
6. How much is kilo of pears?

5

on / at / in · Когда?

📌 on the weekend (амер.) / at the weekend (брит.) — оба верны, но in the weekend — нельзя!
Также: on Saturday, in the morning, at 5 o'clock.

Нажми на правильный предлог. В №1 и №4 правильных ответов два — выбери любой!

1. We go to the market the weekend.
2. I play football Saturdays.
3. Mum cooks pancakes the morning.
4. Do you relax the weekend?
5. The market opens 8 o'clock.
6. My birthday is Monday.

6

How old или How long?

📌 How old — только про возраст (сколько лет кому-то). How long — как долго что-то длится / происходило.
1. are you? — I'm ten.
2. does it take to get to school? — It takes 5 minutes.
3. is your brother? — He is seven.
4. is the film? — Two hours.
5. did you live in Moscow? — For five years.
6. is your grandma? — She is seventy.

7

tell или tall?

📌 tall — высокий (My dad is tall). tell — говорить, рассказывать (Tell me!). Одна буква — большая разница!
1. My dad is very .
2. Please me the truth.
3. Can you me the price?
4. The building is very .
5. How are you?
6. me about your weekend!
